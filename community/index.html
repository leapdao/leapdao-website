Get Involved!

As an open source software community operating a global public utility, LeapDAO thrives from the efforts of its members. Whatever your interests and skills are, there is ample opportunity to make the live of Dapps better!

Contribute Code
  • Check out GitHub issues with the tags Good first issue or bounty.
  • Report a bug and find a solution for it.
  • Propose new features or solve design challenges.
Contribute Support
  • Facilitate holacracy meetings or be a secretary.
  • Help writing documentation.
  • become a bounty gardener or reviewer.

Leap Peeps

Leap Peeps are members who have made a significant contribution to the community and have given Dapps a happy long live. Perks of being a Leap Peep:

  • Get memed by our Memezier!
  • Be in the ins about latest features.
  • That warm and fuzzy feeling.
