LeapDAO

Scaling for decentralized apps

Smart, easy and secure platform using Plasma that enables scalability and performance on Ethereum.

Why use LeapDAO

The current capacity of Ethereum network is fairly limited in its transaction throughput. LeapDAO is increasing this capacity and thus the scope of possible dApps in the ecosystem through the development of Plasma Leap. This will enable decentralized applications with thousands of users at the same time.

Key advantages

We are here to provide a unique solution which, in contrast to others, combines scalability, easiness and security - all in one.

Smart and fast

Up to 4000 transactions per second (500 TPS proven).
Integration into the Ethereum blockchain.

Easy on-boarding

Just need to create a LeapDAO wallet - contracts can pay for gas (initial tokens not required).

Security in mind

Secured by PoW of Ethereum mainnet.
